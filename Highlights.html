1989

Matt and Trey meet in college.


Their first film, "Giant Beavers of Southern Sri Lanka", is created.

1992

"American History", Trey's first construction paper animation, is created. He receives a student award for it.


"The Spirit of Christmas (1992)" is created. It establishes the foundation for the show. It is used as a Christmas card.

1993

"Cannibal! The Musical" is created.

1995

"The Spirit of Christmas (2005)" is created. The main four boys in South Park now exist.

1997

The South Park pilot/first episode is aired.

1998

BASEketball is created. Matt and Trey act as the main characters.


The first South Park game is created.


Matt and Trey receive their first Emmy Award for Outstanding Animated Program.

1999

South Park: Bigger, Longer, and Uncut comes out.

2011

"The Book of Mormon", Matt and Trey's first theatre musical, comes out. They win an award for it.


6 Days to Air comes out. It's an in-depth interview with Matt, Trey, and others who work for South Park.

2004

The Stick of Truth comes out. It is their first big game.

South Park: The Fractured but Whole comes out.

South Park: Snow Day comes out.
